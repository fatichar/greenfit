Replacing dairy works best when each swap is chosen for the job. Milk can be replaced with soy milk, oat milk, or peanut milk. For protein, fortified soy milk is usually more useful than oat milk.

Curd can be replaced with peanut curd or soy curd. Paneer can be replaced with tofu or tempeh. Ghee can be replaced with neutral oils for cooking, or nut and seed pastes when richness is more important than the ghee flavor.

Always check labels for fortification, protein content, added sugar, stabilizers, and source-dependent ingredients. GreenFit uses confidence levels because many products need brand confirmation.
