Omega-3 is a family of fats. ALA comes from foods like flax, chia, walnuts, and some oils. DHA and EPA are the long-chain forms commonly found in fish oil and algal oil.

For people avoiding fish, algal DHA/EPA is the most direct supplement option. When comparing products, look for the actual DHA and EPA amount, not only total oil weight.

Heavy metal reports and batch testing matter for trust. If reports are missing, GreenFit does not assume a product is unsafe, but the confidence level should stay lower.
